The Journey

I'm Safwan Nasir, an Artificial Intelligence Engineer specializing in Computer Vision and Large Language Models. My journey into AI began when I built my first neural network from scratch. The moment I saw it recognize handwritten digits.

Today, I focus on building production-ready AI systems that combine the power of Vision Transformers with real-time streaming architectures. I've had the privilege of working with startups and enterprises to deploy AI solutions that actually make a difference.
